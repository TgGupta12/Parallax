Almonds

Almonds, the versatile and nutrient-packed nuts, have captivated cultures around the world for centuries. Botanically known as Prunus dulcis, almonds are native to the Middle East and Mediterranean region but are now cultivated globally, particularly in California, USA, which produces about 80% of the world's almonds
